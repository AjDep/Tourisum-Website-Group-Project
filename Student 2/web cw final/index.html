Sri Lanka

.. Sri Lanka ..

Sri Lanka is an island country located in the Indian Ocean, south of India.
Its strategic location has played a crucial role in its history,
making it a hub for trade and commerce for centuries.Its position on key maritime trade routes between Europe and Asia has made it a crossroads for various cultures and religions.
The island's unique location also makes it a popular tourist destination, with stunning beaches, lush forests, and an array of wildlife.
Despite its small size, Sri Lanka boasts a rich history and cultural heritage that continues to attract visitors from around the world.
